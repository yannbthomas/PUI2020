ABOUT BUN BUN

Our mission is to provide the best — it always has been. Even upon our establishment in 1837, Bun Bun has dominated Pittsburgh’s bakery scene. For centuries, Bun Bun has prided itself in pushing the bounds of bun making. Little makes us happier than a pristine baked good. Where is square one? Well, it all starts with the bun.

Our master chefs undergo years of rigorous training before being accepted to our chef’s residence program. From there, we hone their skills until only the very best remain. Which is what we offer you — the top of the line. Take your time and look around our site, we welcome any questions that you might have.
